My Solution

While the number of homeless animals in The Bahamas increases daily, the number of organizations
that are available, to help these animals find a home, remains the same. In order to facilitate Bahamian organizations such as The Bahamas National Trust and The Bahamas Humane Society in the process
of finding, rescuing and rehabilitating homeless(or stray) animals, I decided to open a pet store and
animal shelter.

D's Animal Shelter

D's Animal Shelter will be a pet store, animal shelter and animal adoption center that will act as a bridge
between the Bahamian community (including those who own animals and those who do not own animals)
and the animal control organizations of The Bahamas.

Services

D's Animal Shelter will provide services such as:

  • Sale of Pet Food
  • Sale of Animal Toys
  • Animal Adoption Facilities
  • Animal Rehabilitation Centre for injured sick and stray animals
  • Stray/Missing Animal Hotline
  • Volunteer Opportunities
  • Fundraisers








Mission

D's Animal Shelter will be a non-profit organization based in Nassau, Bahamas. Its sole purpose
will be to help animals in need and to provide homes for animals who otherwise didn't have any.
It will act as an animal shelter, pet supplies store and pet adoption centre and will fight for justice,
fairness and humane treatment for all living creatures. The motto will be: 'Rescue, Recuperate,
Rehabilitate'. We will find hurt, sick and injured animals and take them in. We will first find out
what's wrong with them and do whatever we can to help them. However, certain animals will
require special attention and treatment, so they will be sent to either The Bahamas National Trust
or The Bahamas Humane Society.
